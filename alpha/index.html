For players about to explore the Alpha version of our game, here's a list of known bugs we're actively working on:

  1. Donuts Near the Sink Bug: Donuts next to the sink are not moving as intended.
  2. Camera Focus Issue: After the player character drowns in the sink, the camera fails to refocus on the player correctly. This is marked as a top priority issue.
  3. Body Disappearance Near Cashier: There's a critical bug where the player's body disappears when near the cashier.
  4. Camera Clipping with Rotating Table: The camera has been reported to clip through rotating tables, identified as a critical issue.
  5. Jump Mechanic Glitch: Players might experience issues with the jump mechanic not functioning properly.
