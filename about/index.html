About Unbreaded

"Unbreaded" springs from a small, passionate team of indie developers, bonded over a shared love for meaningful narratives and innovative gameplay.

Inspired by the charm of artisan baking and its resilience, our game crafts a story around the struggle against the encroachment of impersonal industrialization. The development journey has been one of creativity, collaboration, and dedication to bringing our vision to life.

"Unbreaded" is more than a game to us; it's a heartfelt tribute to tradition, craft, and the enduring spirit of those who create with passion.
