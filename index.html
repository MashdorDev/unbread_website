What is Unbread?

"Unbread" is not just a game; it's a journey through a heartwarming yet thrilling saga. Crafted with love and a sprinkle of magic, this narrative-driven adventure tells the story of a gingerbread man brought to life by a passionate chef. In the face of adversity, our doughy hero rises to challenge the corporate giants aiming to devour the essence of traditional baking.
